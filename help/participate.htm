How can I participate?

 

To enquire about joining the Semester of Code as either a new organisation or academic institution, you need will need to join the following mailing list.

https://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/cgi-bin/webadmin?A0=VALS-SOC

Send an email to the list introducing yourself and describing what you wish to achieve. Successful organisations and institutions will be sent a code which will allow you to self register in the system.

Students can participate only by being invited by an existing institution within the system. It is the responsibility of your academic supervisor to invite you by email and give you the code to signup.
